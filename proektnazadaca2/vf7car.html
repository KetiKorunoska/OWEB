VF7-Car

New model

Description

With a daringly futuristic design, the compact VinFast VF 7 combines dynamic, avant-garde exterior styling with a spacious, driver-focused, tech-savvy cabin experience for the ultimate EV ride.

The VF 7 comes packed with Advanced driver assistance systems including Highway assist (level 2), Lane centering assist, Adaptive cruise control, Emergency lane keep assist

The modern and sporty exterior is highlighted by signature front and rear light bar with LED effects, smooth body panels with flush door handles, a bold A-wing front apron, and an aggressive rear design.

With up to 348 hp, 368 lb-ft of torque, and access to more than 90% coverage of public chargers throughout North America, the thrilling VF 7 propels you from coast to coast. Experience city drives and cross-country road trips like never before, with access to charging solutions with Level 2 AC and Level 3 DC fast charging no matter where you are.
